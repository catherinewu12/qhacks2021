Meeting the needs of our community, one donation at a time.


Our Mission

Tons of clothing items needlessly end up in thrift stores and landfills. Here at weshare, our goal is to avoid waste and get clothes to those who need the them the most.

1

Your Part

We need you! There are tons of individuals out there who need help getting the essential clothing they need. Help our community. Clean out your closet and find clothing articles you no longer need. Check out what is in need by others around you.

2

Our Part

Here on weshare, we will show you where your items are most needed. With the location information and drop-off times provided, we encourage you to donate where it counts.

3

The Result

With your help, we are able to provide people with more needed clothing articles than ever before. Thank you for helping out the community and our environment!

Our Success

weshare wouldn't exist without your help! Together, our impact is bigger than we could imagine.
